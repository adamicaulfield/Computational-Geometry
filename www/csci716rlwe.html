CSCI716 Project: Homomorphic Encryption & Computational Geometry

Pedagogical Aid & Demo of Homomorphic Algorithm

For this project, I was previously looking into a method to demonstrate Ring-LWE with computational geometry algorithms. However I have discovered that there is more relation between the two areas in the othe direction: using Ring-LWE schemes to homomorphically evaluate comp-geo algorithms Because of this, I will be implementing a homomorphic planaer point location algorithm Results will be demonstrated and explained here
